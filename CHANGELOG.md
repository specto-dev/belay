## 0.3.0 - 2020-11-20

### Added

- Global expectations.
- Locally-handled expectations.
- Expectation blocks.
- Built-in expectation handlers:
  - `Continue`
  - `Return`
  - `ReturnLast`
  - `Throw`
- Custom expectation handlers.
